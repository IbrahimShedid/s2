About HealtTag

HealthTag is a Cloud based platform that empowers healthcare providers and their customers in Egypt doing their daily operations and connecting them with each other through a patient centered smart solution.

HealthTag is the first Product of Bypa-ss for healthcare IT solutions.

Our Goal: HealthTag is just “Empowering who cares”

Services

Care Directory:

Search, Book and find your best healthcare providers. Fully customizable profile to reach more patients.

PHR:

upload, categorize and search any type of medical records for you and add family members you care about. Use Our Smart HealthTags for easy access. HealthTag for Care Provider

fully functioning Electronic Health Recording System (Create, Manage, Exchange and print any type of records) Diagnose Faster, More Patients in less time

Scheduling and Billing:

real-time, highly customizable, and integrated scheduling and billing systems to help running your clinic smoothly .

HealthTag for Enterprise:

tailored Solutions for Enterprises (Health insurance, Schools, Universities, clubs …etc.) Use our Smart HealthTags for easy access. induction Packages are available.
